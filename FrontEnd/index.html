Wuthering Waves is an open-world action RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world scarred by the mysterious Calament. Players take on the role of a Rover, exploring a vast, dynamic environment, uncovering hidden secrets, and battling powerful foes with a fast-paced, skillful combat system. With fluid traversal mechanics and deep storytelling, the game offers an immersive journey of survival, discovery, and self-determination.
