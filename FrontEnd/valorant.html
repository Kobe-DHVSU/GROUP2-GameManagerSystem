About This Game

Genre: Shooting, Tactical Shooter, Hero Shooter

Valorant, is a free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Released in 2020, it blends precise gunplay with unique character abilities, creating a competitive and strategic experience. The game features two teams of five players, each taking turns as attackers and defenders, with the objective to plant or defuse a spike (bomb) or eliminate the opposing team. Each agent in the game has a specific set of abilities, including utilities like smoke grenades, flashes, or healing powers, encouraging players to strategize and play to their strengths. Valorant emphasizes teamwork, coordination, and mechanical skill, making it a favorite in the esports and competitive gaming communities.
